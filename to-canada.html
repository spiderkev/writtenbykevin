To Canada!

Kevin

For our English class this week, we watched the movie "DEAD POETS SOCIETY". It inspired me to some extent, and urged me back to Poetry!

This snowy Thursday morning, before my afternoon chemistry class, I thought of writing a poem, of this thing that changed my life.

Finally came the day, when our immigration process was through. After we had our visa's at hand, we started breaking the news.

We left Goa at the start of July, to Mumbai for a week, through Moscow to Toronto to my Uncles place, to spend a couple of weeks.

Belongings, family and friends.. we had to leave, to go through as we planned, to seek and live an unknown life, in this foreign land.

At Toronto we saw the CN tower, Naigra falls and had parties and fun, but then we flew to Calgary, and hence a new life has begun.

My uncle from USA and another priest, without their help we'd never be... we also met some nicest Calgarians, who even gave us things for free!

We got a comfortable accommodation, close to all amenities, its not shared, therefore rent is high, we pay $1200 + utilities.

Jobs are in plenty here, but a Good one is hard to find. My parents have jobs and my brother as well, and I work part time.

I am doing some upgrading for a year or two, before I get to university. but studies are very easy here, it shouldn't be a problem for me.

Ive already witnessed the extreme weather, a low of -18 degrees Celsius! Ive seen rain, snow and hail as well, but the worst we've yet to experience...

OH! I'm late for my chemistry class... "We're fine so far" - that's all i say. the present seems good and the future, bright and I pray it stays this way...
