New Year 2010

Kevin

It's past 12 and I'm down, on this street, with no where to go and no one to meet. I go to the market to get something to eat. I'm wondering about where i`ll sleep...

New Years come, and I was to celebrate. Go to church, and then party out late. But I'm outside the church gates: with a vada pav in my hand, and boredom on my face - I lost my cell... had no way to communicate...

Mass is over, and I'm looking for a phone. I down a coffee, just to keep me goin! I was about to give up... and head home: when i spot one, across the road.

I call up a buddy. I need a ride. and he meets me in 5! We go to the beach n meet up with the guys. And then we party!... and time flies by...

It was half past 6 when i went for the bus. It took me an hour, but I was home at last. I was too sleepy - to do anything else. I just dropped my bag and crashed to rest.
