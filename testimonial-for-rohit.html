Testimonial for Rohit

Kevin

T'was my first day of college, for the course Math 20 Pure, As i walked in I searched, for someone to sit near...

I strode to the corner end, its my favorite place to sit. I looked to my right, and there was ROHIT...

I introduced my self to him, n he introduced himself to me, and as we kept on talking, I learnt we're from the same country!!

This guy dreams real big; got lots he plans to do. but Ive not known him long enough to see them coming through...

He does have the brain for things, but doesnt study quite a bit. he'd have one eye on the book he's readin and the other on a chick.

Anyway he is a funny, friendly fellow, kind and caring and crazy to the core. He is the type of guy you`d wanna meet, the type you`d wanna know...
