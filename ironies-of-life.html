Ironies of Life

Kevin

Is this world becoming better? or is it becoming worse...? Will technology help our future? or would it be a curse...?

Have the wars in the past achieve its desired goal? or will history repeat itself...? and hence new problems unfold...

...

When in fear, I think of peace. Its in darkness, I value light. Being in the cold, I appreciate warmth. When in wrong, I learn the right.

If somethings missing, I realize its importance. When I'm broke, I value wealth. After failing, I learn to succeed. Being ill, I think of health.

In a crowd, I feel my loneliness. When I'm lost, I find my self. Too much sleep, drains my stamina; after doing nothing, I'd seek to rest.

When I'm troubled, I think of the troubled. After ones gone, I value their presence. This happy life, is filled with sorrow. There is poverty, in the midst of abundance.

When its hard, I seek God, when its not, prayer has no place. Human nature, drowns me in sin; internal conflict, will always be faced...

If I was perfect, I'd be too proud; its mistakes, that make me humble. Earthly pleasures, lead to my downfall... What would life be - without problems or trouble...?
