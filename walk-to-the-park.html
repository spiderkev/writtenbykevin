Walk to the park

Kevin

The air inside was heavy, and a bit too warm. Couldn't find no reason to remain there at home.

So i got my stuff, slipped on flip flops, collected the keys, and locked up.

Outside was much cooler, air much fresher, sun shining brightly, beautiful weather!

I took it all in, and... let it out... then walked down the road and towards the park.

Was a 20 minute walk, so i got some chips n coke to keep me company, along Freshwater road.

The parks are gorgeous this time of the year. and was gleaming life... when i arrived there.

Was early sunday evening, n people living their leisure, dogs playing fetch, n toddlers shouting laughter.

I strolled through the green, settling down my mind, n eased into a park bench - the nicest I could find.

I laid down my head, over my palms, wore a quiet smile, and felt myself calm...
