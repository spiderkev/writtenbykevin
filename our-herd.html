Our Herd

Kevin

We are a herd, we follow the masses that define the terms to which we practice, and strive to be what everyone else is, and ignore the truths by numbing our senses, just to fit in with our bros and our lasses.

We cloth our bodies with jewels and crosses, yet we're hungry, we live in darkness, we ignore our wins but count our losses, satisfaction is our temporary status.

And those people outside our boundaries, they have no name no identities, we trade their sweat to build our economies, and yet of them is what our fear is.

And the media, they don't stay true, they don't show what they're not supposed to, they're no service, they're a business too. Profit is their point of view.

And Entertainment, it's a funny word, it is amusing, obscure and absurd. it distracts us from our world... it kills our time and it drives our herd.
