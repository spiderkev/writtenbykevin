Burst!

Kevin

i see no light on a sunny day, I hear no sound on the freeway, I feel no hunger when i don't eat, I feel tired when i sleep.

You think I’m tough, but I’m not, I’m weak. I'm physically strong, but I can't take this heat.

I have no love or self respect; I have no past, just regret: I'm a king of confusion. I’m a slave of emotion. I’m a dupe of depression.

I hear no music when i hear a tune, I have no life outside my room, I set my clock for 11 am, i get up, and then sleep again.

I get no rest on a quiet night; I fall away when I outta fight: I'm a king of confusion. I’m a slave of emotion. I’m a dupe of depression.

You think I'm fearless, but I’m not, I'm scared. I look bold, but I'm timid instead.

My reality is faulty, a bland - a tasteless story: I'm a king of confusion. I’m a slave of emotion. I’m a dupe of depression.
