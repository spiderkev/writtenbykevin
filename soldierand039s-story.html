Soldier&#039;s Story

Kevin

I am proud of my country and I fight to protect her. Serving her is my career; dying for her is an honour.

Patriotism is my inspiration. The citizens are my family. Its my duty to fight for them, for a better world of harmony.

I spent my youth in training, and now, my country calls: to represent her in battle for a clear, yet unclear cause.

It's been a while now in this foreign land where justice no longer presides. Its a different world - a sophisticated world, but my doubts suggest otherwise.

War is about bravery, courage, and a sacrifice for triumph and freedom. My mind understands this theory of glory but why is my heart heavily burdened... ?

...

It seemed simple in theory but so difficult in practice. I'm confused in belief and fading in conscience. I'm killing for a living, yet its humane in kind. He is dying for a cause quite similar to mine. Tears of grief flow down my eyes of enmity. It's not death i fear. It's this dawning reality...

___ Every day, five U.S. soldiers try to kill themselves. 2,100 soldiers tried to commit suicide in 2007. 99 US soldiers killed themselves last year, the highest in 26 years. The suicide rate among Canada's soldiers doubled from 2006 to 2007. War for Peace?
