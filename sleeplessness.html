Sleeplessness

Kevin

dunno why im up so late, its 3 am and im wide awake; and not for any legit sake just watching my thoughts resonate

my thoughts move like shooting stars start off great then eventually mar leaving darkness and mental scars a waste of time, its what they are

doing nothing is good; its refreshing doing nothing productive - now that's consuming cause the mind ain't still; its still spinning with thoughts recycled - having no new meaning

boredom is a spiritual disease you feel fine, but not at ease ur self is void and hard to please u want away, but have no means

its an illness with no medicine the human mind cannot do nothin if it has no space for growing it will lead to insanity and depression

and its crazy living that way expecting nothing from each new day ur inner balance in disarray knowing well uve gone astray

ah! well now that ive had my boredom fed by slipping into this philosophical tread this rhyme freshened my torment head Im drowsy now... im goin back to bed

-- lol it worked... had a sound sleep after i scribbled this one...
