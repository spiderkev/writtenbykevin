My Newsletter, Jan 2011

Kevin

The engine is roaring but everyone is quiet, and its dark around me, except for my reading light.

I'm on a greyhound bus, 'cause i couldn't get a flight; my destination - Vancouver, and i`ll be riding all night.

I thought I'd take this time to write you`ll another letter; as some of you`ll know, I do this every quarter.

I have moved away for studies, I think I had sent you`ll that memo... its just a 2 hour ferry from Vancouver, the city is called Nanaimo.

Nanaimo is a small city with a population of about 90k. It's a bit quiet, with not much to do, but i like it that way.

It's a coastal city on an island, with a relatively warmer weather, a good place for a retiree or a nature lover.

Anyway, I like its laid-back vibe, especially at it's university, and I have been doing well there so far so I think its working out for me.

My last semester was great! and i was sad to see it end, 'cause I enjoyed all the classes I took, and my results were good as well.

I don't work part-time anymore, and don't expect to next sem either, so I`ll just be taking 4 core courses, and try to intern in the summer.

I recently moved into an apartment there, and said good bye to my dorm, but I spent my last few weeks in Calgary, I had some quality time at home!

We had a wonderful Christmas and New Years - they were both memorable events. Actually my whole holiday at home was amazing, it didn't have any dull moments.

I should probably close soon, for this rhyme is growing fast... Do write back to me now, whenever you`ll have a chance.

Also, send me your latest email address, since my address book needs updating. I don't wanna use Facebook for this no more, it doesn't allow mass messaging.

Alright then, time to switch off my light and put down my pen. My seasons greetings to you, and have a happy 2011!
