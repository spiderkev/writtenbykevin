Light Vision

Kevin

I'm in darkness; I'm lost; I'm running; I'm being chased; I'm bruised; I'm afraid; I'm being chased; I'm running.

I'm searching, to find a place; to hide; to get away. I'm searching, for a place, to hide, to embrace.

I can't see. I don't know! What to want? Where to go? I want to find, a place, where there is light; where I'm safe.

I can't see. I have no way! of knowing, that I've got away; of knowing, that I've found this place; of knowing, that I'm safe.

I give up. I give up! I turn around; I wait. I can't find it. I'll face, whatever is on my chase.

I see, darkness. I hear, silence. I sense, stillness. I open my eyes.
