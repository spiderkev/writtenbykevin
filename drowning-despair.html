Drowning Despair

Kevin

(Verse 1) He lies on his bed, in the darkness of his room; staring at a void, through a mist of gloom. His mind is overcast, by an emptiness; the shadows of a haze of thoughts he obsesses.

(Chorus 1) (He says) "I need that drink in me. I need respite. I want to quite this moment. I want to kill this plight."

(Verse 2) Her breath is swift and hollow; and barely fills her lungs. She lies there suffocating, ruing what has become.

(Chorus 2) (She says) "I need that smoke within me. I need to feel alive. I want to quite this moment. I want to kill this strife."

(Chorus 3) (He says) "I need that drug in me. I need respite. I want to quite this moment. I want to sleep tonight."
