The Pledge

Kevin

(Verse 1) I may not walk on water; I may not have wings, But I will not drown either; I will learn to swim.

I may not know the future; I may be lost for now, But I will find my moment; Time will show me how.

(Chorus) Wheres the sense of adventure? in hiking a plotted trail? Why follow society? and stash your dreams away. Wheres the sense of adventure? in walking on solid ground? You can only leave a footprint, on a path softened with doubt.

(Verse 2) I'll not be Black and White, but be a shade of Grey. I`ll not be afraid to fall, or waste another day. I may face dark and silence; At times be led astray, But I will not be afraid, to find another way.

(Bridge) I must be different; be heard with just a whisper, 'Cause if I don't, then, Wheres the sense of adventure?

(Chorus) Wheres the sense of adventure? in hiking a plotted trail? Why follow society? and stash your dreams away. Wheres the sense of adventure in walking on solid ground? You can only leave a footprint, on a path softened with doubt.

(Verse 3) Pain doesn't hurt, if it doesn't harm. It only separates a dream, from an ambition.

So leave your mold behind, and go follow your heart. 'Cause you can only leave a footprint, on a path softened with doubt.
