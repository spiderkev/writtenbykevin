Today, Yesterday and Tomorrow

Kevin

Decisions grow and you feel low, and pressing thoughts they abrupt your flow, and the road is steep and your hurting deep. You got nothing left to make that leap.

If things aren’t going your way, step back, but don’t walk away, ‘cause nothing is here to stay; tomorrow is another day.

You look back; you see regret, failure, mistakes you recollect, different goals you shoulda set, your thoughts - your choices, they were mislead.

If things didn’t go your way, let go, its okay, ‘cause nothing is here to stay; today is a new day.

With today’s pace, tomorrow is a distant place, it’s dark and desolate. And you contemplate, the moves to make to help mitigate the uncertainty of fate...

What if things don’t go your way? let go, don’t fall away, ‘cause nothing is here to stay; Fight tomorrow's battles tomorrow and today's battles today.
