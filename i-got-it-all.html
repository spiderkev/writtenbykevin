I Got It All!

Kevin

I got the family, I got the love, I got the shelter, the blessings from above.

I got the body, I got the mind. I got a character, of a unique kind.

I got the education, I got the talent, My physical and mental abilities, are very well balanced.

I got the luxuries, I got the wealth, got peace of mind, and I keep good health.

I got all I could imagine, but I don't have satisfaction...
